About Us

Hospitality is a generous reception; it is the devotion and diligence one has towards their guest. Festal Hospitality Group is practicing this art of graciousness toward guests whole-heartedly. Festal is a leading Wedding Hospitality Agency based in Jaipur, India. Established in 2013, Festal has emerged as one of the most polished and prime Wedding Hospitality Agency. We provide services extensively in India and now have global reach as well. We symbolize the fondness and affection a host has towards its guest. Festal’s dictum goes by our ancient beliefs, which is “AtithiDevo Bhava” meaning “Guest is God”, we always deliver exceptional services and maintain utmost decorum. We strive for perfection and bestow inarticulate services on our guests. Our focus is always on our clients and their vision and how we can give them the perfect blend of tailor-made personal affairs and white collared assistance.

About

Founder

Coming from a lively, culturally abundant and generous city, Akanksha was introduced to the gracious warm-hearted welcomes, virtuous “Khama Ghani” and benevolent way of helping and treating guests. Later in her life she completed her study in Event Management and gravitated towards the wedding hospitality sector. She applied her learning and inspiration to the best of her ability and built a team of young and proficient managers, who share her vision to take the industry to the next level.

Meeting new people and bonding over their interests and beliefs inspires Akanksha, weddings are not just weddings for her; it's a fairy-tale come true, a once-in a lifetime experience, a wonderful story shared and experienced by your friends and family. For her, her clients are family, and their guests are as precious to her as to them. No details are small for Akanksha; her team makes sure that your event goes smoothly and hassle-free.

Our Services

Vendor Management

We enable clients to organize, cost cut and manage all vendors during the event. We bridge the gap between the two and always make sure that every aspect is taken care of.

Ritual Management

Rituals are very important for every Indian wedding, we make sure that all your needs are met in that department as well. Bringing necessities to taking care of every detail and sentiment, Festal helps you in every part of your celebration.

R.S.V.P.

(répondezs'ilVousplaît) Festal always takes the RSVP very personally, we never let the guest feel like they are not talking to one of the family members.

Hospitality Management

Managing and planning all the details from guest arrivals to departure. Once your guests arrive it's our responsibility that all their needs are met from luggage tagging to check-ins to conveying them information regarding the event, by being available to them round the clock.

Client Servicing

Taking care of the needs of our clients before, during and after the event. Fulfilling their expectations and converting their vision into a reality.

Logistic Management

Every Event requires a game plan. Managing Travel & Logistics lays down the groundwork for us to build your smooth wedding celebration. Festal makes sure that the arrivals and departures of your guests are effortless and well-ordered.

Designing and Printing Wedding Stationery

Wedding Stationery is an inseparable part of a Wedding. Festal design and conceptualize all your wedding stationery as per your taste and the theme of the wedding.

Artist management

Entertainment is another integral part of any celebration. We have a vast portfolio of very talented and famous artists. We connect and manage Artists for weddings as per the client’s choice.
